HC Truck Licence

HC - Heavy Combination

Drive a prime mover and semi-trailer combination. Includes all HR, MR and LR vehicles.

Eligibility

Hold an HR licence for at least 12 months. Complete the Heavy Vehicle theory test with Qld Transport.

What to Bring on the Day

  • Queensland Driver's Licence
  • Closed in footwear

Discounts for multiple bookings and group bookings — please contact us for a quote.

Medical Conditions

If you have any of the following conditions you will need to obtain a medical certificate stating you are OK to drive for the class of licence you are applying for before starting any training: blackouts, diabetes, eye or vision problems, heart disease, neurological conditions (dementia, stroke, seizures or epilepsy), psychiatric disorders, sleep disorders, alcohol or drug dependency.
