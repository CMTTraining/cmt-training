MC Truck Licence

MC - Multi Combination

Drive road trains and B-double combinations. The highest class of heavy vehicle licence. Includes all HC, HR, MR and LR vehicles.

Eligibility

Hold an HR or HC licence for at least 12 months. TMR accepts the current industry standard for delivery of TLIC4006 Drive MC Vehicle for a class HR driver licence holder as being at least 2 days (16 hours) training and assessment.

What to Bring on the Day

  • Queensland Driver's Licence
  • Closed in footwear
  • CUSI number

Medical Conditions

If you have any of the following conditions you will need to obtain a medical certificate stating you are OK to drive for the class of licence you are applying for before starting any training: blackouts, diabetes, eye or vision problems, heart disease, neurological conditions (dementia, stroke, seizures or epilepsy), psychiatric disorders, sleep disorders, alcohol or drug dependency.
