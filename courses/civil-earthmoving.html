Civil Construction Earthmoving Course

Comprehensive earthmoving training covering multiple machine types with CMT Training, Cairns' most trusted and professional training school.

  • Rolling enrolment means getting on the training site quicker
  • 100% Online theory (printed theory available) — less time off work!
  • Practical training is on a live site, doing real jobs when available
  • 5 (RII) civil construction machinery tickets for $2,100 with our "Totally Diggin' It!" special
  • Add Mining Operations ticket per machine for only $100 per ticket more!

We Can Come to You

If you need any earthmoving tickets, industry cards or WHS certificates, we can come to you. We service all over FNQ (Far North Queensland). Call our friendly staff on 07 4242 1135 for more info.

Available Equipment

Choose from up to 10 types of earthmoving equipment:

  • Excavator (RIIMPO320F)
  • Skid Steer / Bobcat (RIIMPO318F)
  • Front End Loader (RIIMPO321F)
  • Dozer (RIIMPO323E)
  • Roller (RIIMPO317F)
  • Articulated Haul Truck / Moxy (RIIMPO337E)
  • Backhoe (RIIMPO319E)
  • Water Cart (RIIMPO316E)
  • Integrated Tool Carrier (RIIHAN311F)
  • Tractor (RIIMPO315E)

Prerequisites

  • Minimum 17 years of age to sit the assessment
  • Proficient in English
  • Phone, iPad or computer with camera if doing online theory

A short Language, Literacy, Numeracy and Digital Literacy test (LLND) will be required.

What to Bring

  • Photo ID
  • Wear sun-smart and job-site ready clothing - steel capped boots, hat, sunglasses, sunscreen
  • Lunch and water/drink
