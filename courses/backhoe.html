Backhoe Ticket

RIIMPO319E Conduct backhoe/loader operations

Conduct backhoe/loader operations. Nationally recognised course for a great versatile machine used in a wide range of applications.

Course Details

Duration1-2 Days
Cost$580 beginners / $480 experienced

Course Locations

TheoryOnline (printed theory available)
Practical76 Lower Freshwater Rd, Kamerunga QLD 4870
Office8 Spoto Street, Woree (Cairns), QLD 4870
Time7:00am - 3:00pm Monday to Friday

Course Outline

This nationally recognised course gives you the skills and knowledge to conduct backhoe/loader operations safely and efficiently on civil construction and earthmoving sites.

  • Legislation and standards applicable to operating a backhoe/loader
  • Risk management, control procedures & preparing for backhoe operations
  • Conducting pre-operational and start-up checks
  • Operating the backhoe/loader and modifying technique to meet changing conditions
  • Selecting, removing and fitting attachments
  • Relocating the machine
  • Carrying out operator maintenance and housekeeping

Assessment type: Theory and Practical

On successful completion you will receive a Statement of Attainment and a plastic industry wallet card issued by CMT Training RTO.

Prerequisites

  • Minimum 17 years of age to sit the assessment
  • Proficient in English
  • A short Language, Literacy, Numeracy and Digital Literacy test (LLND) will be required

What to Bring

  • Photo ID
  • Sun-smart and job-site ready clothing
  • Steel capped boots, hat, sunglasses, sunscreen
  • Lunch and water/drink
