Best Value Industry Training in North Queensland

CMT Training provides heavy vehicle driver training/assessing, forklift training/assessing and other industry training across North Queensland.

We provide a range of courses covering the civil construction, mining and transport sectors. Our courses such as ACDC, chainsaw and side by side utility vehicles also covers the farming sectors.

We have a large fleet of modern vehicles offering the best and most economical way to get your licence as quickly as possible including LR/MR, HR, HC and MC with different type of gearboxes from automatic, synchromesh (single clutch) and non-synchromesh (double clutch 18 speed road ranger).

Our instructors are industry experienced and we offer one-on-one tuition to ensure fast and cost effective results. We pride ourselves on quickly assessing your skill level then customising training to address your individual needs.

CMT Training offers training based in Cairns and Far North Queensland, using Queensland Transport Customer Service Centres at Port Douglas, Mossman, Mareeba, Atherton, Innisfail and other areas.

We offer High Risk Work Licence for Forklift, industry training for Working at Heights, Confined Spaces, Dangerous Goods, White Card and other courses.

On or off site training available. Group discounts apply. Call us on 07 4242 1135 for details.

We are proudly a family owned and operated business.
